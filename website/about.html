About GAKK Consulting

GAKK Consulting is a systems architecture and operational governance firm. We work with FQHCs and community health organizations to build structured operational substrates — audit frameworks, documentation governance systems, intake architectures, and workflow stabilization procedures — that address the conditions producing revenue loss and compliance exposure.

Our Doctrine

We approach every engagement governance-first. That means we do not recommend process changes without first understanding the structural conditions that produced the current state. We build deterministic systems — procedures and frameworks with defined inputs, defined decision logic, and traceable outputs — rather than guidance that depends on individual judgment to hold. Our work is substrate-aligned: every deliverable is designed to function within the operational reality of the organization, not against it.

Why FQHCs

FQHCs operate under payer complexity that most health systems do not face: sliding fee schedules, encounter rate billing, UDS reporting requirements, managed care contract variation, and coverage populations with high eligibility volatility. These conditions create documentation drift, intake instability, and denial patterns that standard revenue cycle approaches are not designed to address. GAKK Consulting's work is built specifically for this environment.

Billing & Revenue Cycle

Billing is not separate from operations — it is the endpoint of every documentation, intake, and eligibility decision made earlier in the encounter lifecycle. When billing fails at scale, the cause is almost never the billing department alone. GAKK Consulting treats revenue cycle performance as a downstream indicator of upstream structural conditions, and our work addresses both: the substrate problems that generate billing failures and the billing governance frameworks needed to manage them once the substrate is stable.

How We Work

The core of our practice is sequencing and architecture. We identify the structural conditions driving operational problems, design the substrate needed to address them, and sequence implementation in a way that is executable within the organization's current capacity. We do not deliver reports that require external interpretation. We deliver frameworks that the organization can operate, audit, and maintain.
